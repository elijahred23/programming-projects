Co-Pilot

What is Co-Pilot?

Co-Pilot is a web application that allows you to create and share your own custom maps. You can use Co-Pilot to create maps for your next road trip, or to share your favorite hiking trails with your friends.

How do I use Co-Pilot?

It's easy! Just click on the "Create a Map" button below to get started.

Create a Map

What can I do with Co-Pilot?

  • Create custom maps
  • Share your maps with friends
  • View maps created by other users
